# Privacy Policy — WalkYourTime

**Developer:** FilBanfi  
**Contact:** filbanfi@hotmail.com

## 1. Overview

WalkYourTime is a privacy-first application. All data generated and used by the app is stored exclusively on your device. No data is collected, transmitted, or shared with any external server or third party by FilBanfi.

---

## 2. Data Collected and Stored

WalkYourTime stores the following data **locally on your device only**, using Android's standard SharedPreferences and/or Room database:

- **Step count data** — recorded via the device's built-in step counter sensor.
- **Screen time and app usage data** — monitored via Android Accessibility Service to enforce app blocking rules.
- **User preferences and settings** — including goals, thresholds, and app configuration.

This data never leaves your device and is never accessible to FilBanfi or any third party.

---

## 3. Permissions Used

WalkYourTime requests the following Android permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Accessibility Service** | Monitors foreground app usage to apply screen time limits based on your step count. |
| **Activity Recognition / Step Counter** | Counts your daily steps to calculate earned screen time. |
| **Post Notifications** | Sends local reminders and alerts about your screen time status. |
| **Request Ignore Battery Optimizations** | Ensures the step counter and blocking service continue to work reliably in the background. |

No permission is used for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond the core app functionality described above.

---

## 4. Third-Party Integrations

WalkYourTime does **not** currently integrate with any third-party health or fitness platform. Future versions may offer optional integration with third-party health and fitness services.

If and when such integrations are added:
- They will be strictly **opt-in**.
- Data shared with those platforms will be governed by their respective privacy policies, not this one.
- FilBanfi will not have access to any data exchanged between WalkYourTime and those third-party services.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

FilBanfi does **not** sell, rent, share, or disclose any user data to third parties. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting services included in the app.

---

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by clearing the app's storage in your device settings, or by uninstalling the app.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

WalkYourTime is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
